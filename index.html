Welcome to My Page! I am a Ph.D student in the Department of Physics, Cotton University, Assam, India. Currently, I am working on Variable Stars. I have completed my Master's from Gauhati University, Assam , India with specialization in Astrophysics & Cosmology.

Research Interests

My research focuses on leveraging variable stars to advance our understanding of stellar properties, galaxy structures, and the Universe, in general. More specifically, it has focused on using the recently obtained archival data in conjunction with theoretical stellar models to get deeper insights into the classical pulsators and their host galaxies. The classical pulsators can also be used as excellent distance indicators as they exhibit strict period-luminosity relations (PLR). I use the PLR to unravel the three-dimensional structure of their host galaxies.
